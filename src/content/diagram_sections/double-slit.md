---
title: "Double Slit — Measurement as Overlap Integral"
diagram: DoubleSlit
order: 12
---

A plane wave ψ = eⁱᵏˣ hits a barrier with two slits. Circular wavefronts emerge from each slit and interfere. At the detection screen, a localized particle detector with Gaussian response of width σ samples the wavefunction through the overlap integral ⟨det|ψ⟩. The measured intensity pattern P(y) depends on both the interference fringe spacing (set by slit separation d and wavelength λ) and the detector resolution σ. A large detector washes out the fringes; a small detector resolves them — the measurement apparatus is part of the physics.
