The fundamental coupled mode system: two modes with propagation constant β, coupling κ, and mismatch Δβ. Set the initial amplitudes and watch the carrier fields propagate while the envelopes show energy exchange. With Δβ = 0 and A₂(0) = 0, all energy transfers to mode 2 at the beat length π/2κ.
