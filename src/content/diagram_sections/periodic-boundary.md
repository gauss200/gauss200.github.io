Two waveguides coupled by a matrix A with the constraint that the initial state equals the final state: ψ(0) = ψ(L). This periodic boundary condition quantizes the allowed propagation constants. Only eigenmodes whose phase accumulation over L is a multiple of 2π form self-consistent standing patterns. Switch between symmetric, antisymmetric, and superposition modes to see how the boundary condition constrains the system.
