Research spanning the full arc of amorphous silicon science — from fundamental measurements of the electronic density of states and metastable defect formation (Staebler-Wronski effect), through hydrogen diffusion mechanisms, to device applications including thin-film transistors for flat-panel displays and X-ray imagers.
